The initial day of #ChefConf 2012 was a great success!  The day included Workshops for Developers and System Administrators and a Hackday event focused on testing all the things.

Download the episode now!

In this episode, Bryan and Nathen sat down with Boyd Hemphill to recap the day.

Some of the things worked on during the Hackday include:

  • "Fixing" an issue with minitest-handler-cookbook
  • Some thoughts on BDD Cookbook writing
  • Some work on Vagrant, Toft, and lxc including the discovery of the Sahara gem.
  • Exciting exploration of testing and refactoring the NTP cookbook.
