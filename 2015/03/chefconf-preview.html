Outline

Chef Conf 2015: Game On! March 31 - April 2 at the Santa Clara Convention Center in Santa Clara, CA.

Day 1: Tuesday, March 31st

  • Workshop - Introduction to Chef: Managing Linux
    • Diversity Scholarship
    • Diversity in the Chef Community
  • Chef Community Summit
    • Open Space Event
  • Welcome Reception Beverages & Snacks provided.
    • 5:30pm - 7:00pm in the Exhibit Hall
  • Chef Meetup @ Ooyala
    • Ooyala

Day 2: Wednesday, April 1st

  • 5K Run
  • Power Yoga with Marilyn Olson Timberman
    • Yoga is located on the 2nd floor foyer, just at the top of the escalator from the Conference Center’s Main Foyer.
  • Keynote Sessions
    • Nathen Harvey
    • Barry Crist and Justin Arbukle
      • Standard Bank: Our DevOps Journey (part 1)
      • Standard Bank: Our DevOps Journey (part 2)
      • Standard Bank: Our DevOps Journey (part 3)
      • Standard Bank: Our DevOps Journey (part 4)
    • Surprise Speaker!!
  • Track Sessions
    • Brian Scott and Jeff LaPlante: Disney DevOps: Automating the Enterprise with Supermarket, Containers and BI
    • Bridget Kromhout: Cooking Up Drama
    • Jamesha Fisher: Securing the Heart of Automated Infrastructure
    • Matt Wrock: Entering the Chef Ecosystem from a Windows Background
  • Panel: Have Your Bets On Open Paid Off? with Cade Metz
  • Sponsors’ Happy Hour
  • After Party at Levi’s Stadium
  • Game Night

Day 3: Thursday, April 2nd

  • Gentle and Slow Yoga with Marilyn Olson Timberman
    • Yoga is located on the 2nd floor foyer, just at the top of the escalator from the Conference Center’s Main Foyer.
  • Keynote Sessions
    • Adam Jacob
    • Jez Humble
    • Nicole Forsgren
  • Track Sessions
    • Jessica DeVita: Automate What You Can, Document What You Can’t and Have the Wisdom to Know the Difference
    • Christine Draper: Serve Meals, Not Ingredients
    • Jon Cowie: [Workflow Design: Extracting Signal from the Noise
    • Harold “Waldo” Grunenwald: Chef Retrospective
    • Mukta Aphale: Baking Docker Using Chef

Other Talks and Information

  • POSHChef
  • PowerShell from the Group Up
  • Windows PowerShell Desired State Configuration (DSC) for Veteran Chefs
  • Networking SDD: Same Stuff, Different Department

  • Thursday Lightning Talks: DevLOLOps - How To Automate Your DevOps Hilarity
