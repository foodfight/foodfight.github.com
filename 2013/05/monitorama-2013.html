Monitorama 2013

May 21st, 2013

Show Date: 29 March 2013

Brandon Burton (@solarce) took a microphone to this year’s Monitorama and was able to record a bunch of DevOps Delicacies for the show. It’s taken Nathen a while to get these edited and ready for release but here they are!

  • Bear on DevOps Consulting with @bear
  • Bethany Erskine of Paperless Post with @skymob
  • Github and Happiness with @wfarr, @jnewland, and @nixgeek
  • Heavywater consulting with @portertech and @webframp
  • John Willis on Docker with @botchagalupe
  • Julian Martinez from Locaweb with @ncode
  • Kyle Kingsbury on Riemann with @aphyr
  • Pete Cheslock on Culture with @petecheslock
  • Sam Kottler with @samkottler
