Alan’s recommended path to get up-to-speed on the STELLA Report:

  1. The best introduction from a DevOps perspective to the concepts covered in the Stella Report is John Allspaw’s talk on Resilience Engineering as DevOps from the 2017 DevOps Enterprise Summit

  2. Second read the Stella Report from the Workshop on Coping With Complexity.

  3. Finally check out DevOpsDays Detroit 2017: Day 1 Keynote by Dr. Richard I. Cook.
